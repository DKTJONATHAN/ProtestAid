Emergency Instructions

If you're injured:

  • Find a safe place away from the conflict
  • Apply pressure to bleeding wounds with clean cloth
  • For tear gas, rinse eyes with clean water or milk
  • Call for medical help using the form or buttons above

If you're arrested:

  • Remain calm and don't resist
  • Demand to see a lawyer immediately
  • You have the right to remain silent - don't sign anything
  • Ask to contact ProtestAid legal team

If you witness an abduction:

  • Note the vehicle details (color, plate, direction)
  • Record video if safe to do so
  • Alert others nearby loudly
  • Immediately contact our network using the form
