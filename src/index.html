About ProtestAid Kenya

Born from the 2023-2024 youth-led protest movements like #RejectFinanceBill2024 and #RutoMustGo, ProtestAid Kenya provides critical support to demonstrators facing police violence, abductions, and medical emergencies.

Our platform is built by activists for activists, prioritizing safety, accessibility, and rapid response in Kenya's high-risk protest environment.

Protest Safety Tips

Essential knowledge for staying safe during demonstrations

Buddy System

Never protest alone. Stay with trusted friends who can help if you're injured or arrested.

Emergency Contacts

Write important numbers on your arm in permanent marker in case your phone is confiscated.

Tear Gas Response

Don't rub your eyes. Rinse with clean water or milk. Move upwind and to higher ground.

Know Your Rights

You have the right to remain silent. Demand to see a lawyer if arrested. Don't resist.
